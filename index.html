Best Towns for Drinking Beer

Maple Shade, NJ

Beer lovers in the small town of Maple Shade, New Jersey can enjoy house-made beer and American comfort fare at Iron Hill Brewery and Restaurant.

Berlin, MD

The town of Berlin, Maryland has a population of fewer than 5,000 people, yet it has a relatively big craft beer selection. Burley Oak Brewing Company crafts some of the best brews on the shore, while Pitt Stop Beer & Wine carries more than 850 different beers.

Hood River, OR

Taos, NM

Drive north of Taos to see the relatively new brewing facilities of Taos Mesa Brewing. The Burger Stand at Taos Ale House serves up top-rated food and experimental beers.
